## 1) Project Objective
Deliver a modern, mobile-first dental appointment booking platform that helps patients discover services, book quickly, and communicate with the practice efficiently.

## 2) Current App Snapshot
The current app already demonstrates:
- Professional homepage with service promotions and clear calls-to-action.
- Services catalog with pricing and treatment durations.
- Step-by-step appointment booking flow.
- Booking management view for upcoming/history appointments.
- Practice profile and location/contact pages.
- Built-in chatbot and timed engagement prompts.
- Responsive design for mobile, tablet, and desktop.

## 3) What Will Be Delivered for Go-Live
To move from demo to production, the launch scope includes:
- Secure user sign-up and sign-in.
- PostgreSQL database integration for users and appointments.
- API backend for authentication and booking operations.
- Email confirmations (and optional SMS reminders).
- Security hardening and account protection controls.
- Production deployment with custom domain and SSL.

## 4) Recommended Go-Live Stack
- Frontend hosting: Vercel
- Backend hosting: Render or Railway
- Database: Supabase Postgres or Neon Postgres
- Email provider: Resend or SendGrid
- Optional SMS reminders: Twilio or Clickatell

Why this stack:
- Fast to deploy and maintain.
- Cost-effective for small to medium clinic traffic.
- Scales as patient volume grows.

## 5) Security and Account Protection
The production version will include:
- Password hashing (Argon2id or bcrypt).
- Email verification.
- Secure session/token handling.
- Rate limiting for sign-in/sign-up endpoints.
- Bot protection on critical forms.
- POPIA-conscious data handling and HTTPS enforcement.

## 6) Timeline
Estimated delivery: 2 to 4 weeks (based on selected package and feedback speed).

Typical sequence:
- Week 1: Backend + database foundation.
- Week 2: Auth + bookings + confirmations.
- Week 3: Security hardening + testing.
- Week 4: Final QA + deployment + handover.

## 7) Investment Summary (ZAR)
Final quote for this project:
- Once-off development, testing, and live production: R5,000
- Monthly maintenance fee: R1,000/month
- Monthly email and social media marketing support: R1,000/month

Value-first pricing note:
- This pricing is intentionally set to deliver more value than cost, with a strong focus on long-term partnership and referral-based growth.

## 8) Business Outcome
This system reduces booking friction, improves patient conversion, and gives the practice a credible digital booking channel that can operate 24/7.
